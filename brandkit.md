## Instrument Serif Usage Rules

### Sizes:

- Hero: 64px–96px
    
- Large Section: 48px–64px
    
- Medium Section: 32px–48px
    

### Line Height:

0.9–1.1

### Weight:

Regular only.  
Italic for emphasis.  
Never bold-heavy.

### Example Structure:

_before_ you build.

India’s Top  
_Ventures_

Welcome to  
BackMyIdea

# 🔟 BRAND DO & DON’T

## DO

✔ Use serif for big statements  
✔ Use mono for numbers  
✔ Keep backgrounds warm  
✔ Use orange with restraint  
✔ Maintain high contrast

## DON’T

✖ Don’t use neon colors  
✖ Don’t add gradients everywhere  
✖ Don’t use playful illustrations  
✖ Don’t mix multiple serif fonts  
✖ Don’t make UI too rounded

---

# 1️⃣1️⃣ ACCESSIBILITY NOTES

Minimum contrast ratio:  
4.5:1

Primary orange on white:  
Passes for large text.

Never use orange for body text.
