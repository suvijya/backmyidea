## 1.4 Product Principles

text

```
1. SIMPLICITY FIRST
   Every feature must be usable by a first-year college 
   student in a Tier 3 city on a budget Android phone.

2. DATA IS THE PRODUCT
   Every interaction generates structured data. Votes, 
   comments, views, shares, funding — all are data points 
   that compound in value.

3. TRUST THROUGH TRANSPARENCY
   Scores are explainable. Algorithms are documented. 
   Investor interest is visible. No black boxes.

4. INDIA-FIRST, NOT INDIA-ONLY
   Designed for Indian founders, Indian investors, Indian 
   public. Vernacular support. UPI integration. Tier 2/3 
   focus. But not geo-blocked.

5. EARN BEFORE YOU ASK
   Give value before asking for money. Free tier must be 
   genuinely useful. Paid features enhance, not gate.

6. COMMUNITY OVER FEATURES
   A vibrant community of 10,000 is worth more than a 
   feature-rich product with 100 users.
```

### SIDE 1 — Founder Dashboard

What a founder does here:

- **Submit an idea** with a structured pitch
    - Problem statement
    - Solution
    - Target audience
    - Stage (just idea / MVP / revenue)
    - Team info
    - Optional: video pitch, prototype link
- **Launch a validation campaign**
    - "Would you use this?" polls
    - Pricing sensitivity surveys
    - Feature priority voting
    - Landing page with waitlist
- **See real-time analytics**
    - Public interest score
    - Demographic breakdown of who validated
    - Sentiment analysis from comments
    - AI-generated validation report
- **Raise micro-funds / donations**
    - People pay ₹50–₹500 to support
    - In return: early access, founding member badge, discounts
- **Investor visibility toggle**
    - Opt-in to be visible to investors
    - AI readiness score shown to investors

---

### SIDE 2 — Public/Consumer Side

What normal people do here:

- **Browse ideas like a feed** (think Product Hunt meets Instagram)
    - By category: fintech, health, food, D2C, etc.
    - By stage: raw idea, building, launched
    - By trending, new, most validated
- **Validate ideas**
    - Quick swipe: "I'd use this" / "Not for me"
    - Detailed: answer founder's specific questions
    - Comment with feedback
- **Support/Donate**
    - Micro-donations (₹10 and up)
    - Get rewards (early access, merch, shoutouts)
    - "Founding Supporter" badge on profile
- **Follow ideas** and get updates
- **Earn reputation**
    - Points for validating, commenting, referring
    - Leaderboard of top validators
    - Eventually: perks, access to exclusive launches

> **KEY QUESTION: Why would the public come here?**  
> This is your hardest problem. You need to gamify it. Think:
> 
> - "Be the first to discover the next Zerodha"
> - Earn points, badges, rewards
> - Sense of ownership ("I validated this before it blew up")
> - Actual monetary rewards if a startup succeeds

---

### SIDE 3 — Investor Dashboard

What investors see:

- **Curated deal flow**
    - Filtered by sector, stage, validation score, location
    - AI-ranked by potential
- **Validation data they can't get anywhere else**
    - "4,200 people said they'd pay for this"
    - "72% of validators are 18-25, Tier 2 cities"
    - Sentiment analysis on public comments
    - Comparison tool: Idea A vs Idea B
- **Founder profiles with credibility scores**
    - Based on: team, progress, public validation, engagement
- **Direct connect**
    - Request a pitch meeting
    - Ask founder specific questions
    - Mark as "watching" / "interested" / "pass"
- **Portfolio tracking**
    - Track startups they've funded through the platform
    - See ongoing validation data post-funding
